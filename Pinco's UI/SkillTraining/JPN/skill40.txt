
0 - 40:  Train at NPC Warrior in New Haven.

40 - 120:

·	Put on a suit of armor to get as much Physical Resistance as possible (the virtue armor set is perfect for the task).

·	Carry as much shield as you can.

·	Find a sheep field (there are several around the world).

·	Attack as many sheeps as you can and let them hit you continuously while wearing a shield.

NOTE: The shield durability and your hit points will goes down fast, so be prepared to heal very fast or to have someone who heals you.
