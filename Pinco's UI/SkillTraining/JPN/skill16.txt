
You need a friend tamer or to have high taming on the character. Then tame the following creatures and discord it. Once you have success cast invisibility on yourself, wait 10 seconds and then discord it again.

0 - 30: Train from an NPC Bard

25 - 55: Dire Wolf

55 - 72: Giant Beetle

72 - 90: Hiryu

90 - 120: Take the Discordance Mastery from Britain Conservatory, then continuously cast Tribulation on yourself.
Having an high Mana Regeneration suit combined with Meditation and possibly Focus will grants a faster training.
